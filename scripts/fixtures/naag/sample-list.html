Multistate Cases — Settlements

Equifax Data Breach Multistate Settlement

September 30, 2019 Settlements

$700 million global settlement with credit-reporting bureau Equifax over the 2017 data breach exposing personal information of 147 million Americans. Joined by 48 states, D.C., and Puerto Rico.

JUUL Labs Multistate Marketing Settlement

September 6, 2022

$438.5 million multistate settlement with JUUL Labs Inc. resolving a two-year, bipartisan investigation into the e-cigarette manufacturer’s marketing and sales practices. Thirty-three states and Puerto Rico joined.

Google Location History Multistate Settlement

November 14, 2022

$391.5 million settlement with Google LLC for misleading consumers about location-data tracking practices on Android and iOS devices. Forty states participated, marking the largest multistate consumer-privacy settlement in U.S. history.

Opioid Distributors Multistate Settlement

February 25, 2022

$26 billion multistate settlement with McKesson Corporation, Cardinal Health Inc., AmerisourceBergen Corporation, and Johnson & Johnson resolving claims related to the marketing and distribution of prescription opioids. Forty-six states joined.

Intuit TurboTax “Free” Filing Settlement

May 4, 2022

$141 million multistate settlement with Intuit Inc. over deceptive marketing of free TurboTax tax-prep services. All 50 states and the District of Columbia participated.

Small Local Charity Settlement

January 12, 2021

$250,000 settlement with regional fundraising company over allegations of consumer fraud. Three states joined.
