Equifax Data Breach Multistate Settlement

Settled: September 30, 2019

$700 million global settlement with Equifax Inc. resolves claims by 48 states, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico arising out of the 2017 data breach that exposed the personal information of approximately 147 million Americans.

Summary

The September 30, 2019 settlement requires Equifax to spend up to $425 million to help people affected by the data breach, including credit monitoring and identity-theft protection. The agreement also requires Equifax to strengthen its data-security program, undergo annual third-party audits, and adopt minimum security standards for sensitive consumer data.
