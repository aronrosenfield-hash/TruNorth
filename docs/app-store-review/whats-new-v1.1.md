# App Store "What's New" — v1.1 (Build 76)

> Build 75 (the launch binary) predates the post-launch data wave, so for users v1.1 is a real feature update: the public-record footprint card, industry disclosure tags, ITEP tax data, and animal-cert scoring are all new. Keep it plain and honest — journalist, not salesperson.

## Primary (paste into App Store Connect → What's New)

```
This update puts more of the public record in your hands:

• Public-record footprint — every brand now shows a snapshot built from government data: greenhouse-gas and toxic emissions, federal tax rate, product recalls, mass layoffs, and more.

• Corporate tax transparency — see a company's 5-year federal effective tax rate and how many of those years it paid $0, sourced from the Institute on Taxation and Economic Policy (ITEP).

• Industry disclosure tags — clear read-only labels when a brand's parent operates in tobacco, fossil fuels, firearms, or alcohol. You decide what matters.

• Cruelty-free & humane certifications now factor into your personalized grade.

• More sources, cleaner data, and accuracy and reliability fixes throughout.

As always: records, not opinions. Every grade traces back to a primary source you can open yourself.
```
