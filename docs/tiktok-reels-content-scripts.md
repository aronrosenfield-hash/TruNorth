# TikTok / Reels Content Scripts — TruNorth

> **Why:** TikTok/Reels have the highest leverage for TruNorth's value prop — the "reveal" moment when you see a brand's actual record translates perfectly to a 15-30 second video.
>
> **Cadence:** Aim for 3 videos in the first week post-PH-launch, then 2/week ongoing. Each video should hit a different angle.
>
> **Format:** Vertical 9:16, captions burned in (most TikTok viewers watch muted), 15-30s, no music license needed if you use TikTok's library.
>
> **Production:** Filmed on iPhone. No edit suite needed beyond CapCut or iMovie.

**Visual notes:**
- Burn captions on screen (CapCut auto-captions)
- Use TikTok's stock "thinking" sound under the reveal moments

## Production logistics

### Filming setup

- iPhone 13+ camera, 4K 30fps
- Natural light from window (no ring lights for founder videos — looks too polished)
- Tripod for hands-free, or just lean phone against a stack of books
- Lavalier mic ($25 on Amazon) makes a huge difference if you're talking to camera

### Editing app

- **CapCut** (free, owned by ByteDance / TikTok) — has auto-captions, easy cuts, music library
- **iMovie** if you prefer Apple — but captions are manual
- Don't over-edit. TikTok rewards "feels real" over "feels produced"

### Captions rule

EVERY video needs burned-in captions. 80%+ of TikTok views happen muted. Use CapCut's auto-caption → review/fix typos → burn in.

### Hashtag strategy

- **Always include**: #ethicalshopping #consciousshopping
- **Match topic**: #climate #labor #politicaldonations #fec #osha (pick 2-3 per video)
- **Trending**: check daily, swap in whatever's trending
- Total: 5-8 hashtags max. More dilutes reach.

### Cadence

- **Week 1 post-launch**: 3 videos (one per 2-3 days). Hit different angles — scan, founder, comparison.
- **Week 2-4**: 2 videos/week. Mix evergreen + reactive (any brand in the news).
- **Ongoing**: 1 video/week minimum to feed the algo.

### Cross-post

Same video → TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts. Different algos pick different winners. Don't watermark — the algos penalize watermarked content from other platforms.

### Tracking

Use UTM links in the bio: `trunorthapp.com?utm_source=tiktok` or `?utm_source=reels`. Wire to PostHog so you know which video drives the most installs.

### What NOT to film

- ❌ Long talking-head explainers (>30s)
- ❌ Outrage-bait at any specific brand (libel risk + algorithmic penalty)
- ❌ "Watch this brand's CEO say something wild" — drama videos go viral but don't convert to app installs
- ❌ Anything that looks like an ad (TikTok kills reach on overly-promotional content)

### The big idea

The data is the differentiator. Every video should end with the viewer thinking: "I want to know what TruNorth says about [brand I just thought of]." That's the install moment.
