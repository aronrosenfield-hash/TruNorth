# L-3 Personal Email Blast — Send Checklist

**Goal:** 10-20 personalized emails to your closest network. Drives the most reliable hour-1 votes on PH launch day (Jun 23 · 2:01 AM CDT).

**Recommended send window:** Today through Wed Jun 11 (gives 12 days before launch — enough to remind them on launch day).

---

## 📋 Recipient strategy

Aim for 10-20 people. Quality > quantity. Each one should be someone who:
- Would actually open + read an email from you
- Trusts your judgment enough to subscribe without overthinking
- Has SOME social/professional footprint where they could re-share on launch day

### Mental categories (target 2-5 per bucket)

**1. Inner circle (5 people min)**
- Spouse / partner
- Parents
- Siblings
- 1-2 lifelong best friends

**2. Former colleagues (5-7 people)**
- Old bosses you still respect each other with
- Smart peers from past companies
- Anyone who saw you build something before and would trust your taste

**3. Founder / startup network (3-5 people)**
- Other indie founders you know
- Anyone in the ethical-tech, consumer-tech, or app space
- Y Combinator / IndieHackers people if any

**4. Adjacent professionals (2-4 people)**
- A journalist or two if you have any contacts
- Anyone in sustainability / ESG consulting
- Lawyers/accountants who'd care about the audit-trail angle

**5. High-influence longshots (1-3 people)**
- Anyone with a real Twitter/LinkedIn following you've ever interacted with
- If you've ever exchanged DMs with a known indie maker, podcaster, or consumer journalist, this is your shot

Aron
Aron@trunorthapp.com

## 🛠️ Send mechanics (Gmail web)

1. Open https://mail.google.com → click **Compose**
2. Put `Aron@trunorthapp.com` in the **BCC** field (so you have a copy + recipients can't see each other)
3. Put ONE recipient in the **To** field (one email per person — don't BCC the whole list)
4. **Subject:** copy the line above
5. **Body:** paste the template, swap `[name]` for their first name
6. Send
7. Repeat for each recipient

Time budget: ~90 seconds per email × 15 recipients = ~25 minutes.

## 🚨 What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't BCC everyone on one email (looks spammy)
- ❌ Don't include attachments (looks suspicious)
- ❌ Don't ask them to "share with their network" yet — that's launch day
- ❌ Don't write more than the template — short is read; long isn't
- ❌ Don't follow up if they don't respond. They saw it. One ping per person until launch.

## What happens if a contact replies "yes, send me TestFlight"

You have two options:
1. **(easy)** Reply with: "Awesome — here's the TestFlight invite link: https://testflight.apple.com/join/[your-code]"
2. **(personal touch)** Reply: "Awesome — sending you a TestFlight invite now. Let me know what you think of [specific thing relevant to them]." Then go to App Store Connect → TestFlight → add their Apple ID email manually.

Option 1 scales. Option 2 deepens the relationship. Use option 2 for the 5 most important contacts.
