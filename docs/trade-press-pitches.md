## Operational notes

### Sequence

1. **Jun 9-13**: research the journalists. Read their last 3 articles. Personalize the opening sentence of each pitch with a reference to their recent work.
2. **Jun 16**: send all 4 pitches. Use Aron@trunorthapp.com (looks professional, DKIM-verified).
3. **Jun 21**: send the one-line nudge to anyone who hasn't replied.
4. **Jun 23**: launch goes live. The pitches you DID land will publish around then.

### Things NOT to do

- ❌ Don't BCC all 4 outlets on the same email
- ❌ Don't pitch the same week as competing products' launches (check Product Hunt calendar)
- ❌ Don't say "this is going to be huge" or "everyone is going to love it" — journalists smell hype
- ❌ Don't ask them to upvote on PH — that's a code-of-ethics nope
- ❌ Don't follow up more than once

### If you land one

- Get the journalist's interview window in the calendar BEFORE the article runs
- Offer to send TestFlight + the underlying data CSV for one company they pick (proves the receipts)
- Add their byline + outlet + publication date to a press section on trunorthapp.com

### If you land zero

- Repeat the cycle 4 weeks later with launch results data ("TruNorth launched at #X on PH, got Y signups in week one. Here's what we learned.")
- That second cycle has way higher hit rate — you have actual numbers to anchor the story.
