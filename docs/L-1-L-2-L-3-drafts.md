## L-3 — Personal email blast (30 min)

**When to send:** **Sunday Jun 22 evening** (so it lands in inboxes Monday morning). Sending Tuesday Jun 23 is also fine but a day earlier converts better.
**How:** Send 10-20 individual emails (NOT a BCC blast — each one personalized in the first line). Use plain text, no fancy formatting.

### Recipient list ideas

- Family + close friends (5-10)
- Former colleagues you still talk to (3-5)
- Anyone who's tested TestFlight already (2-5)
- Mentors / advisors / people who've helped you (3-5)
- ⚠️ Skip: people you haven't talked to in 2+ years (low conversion, feels spammy)

### Email template

```
Subject: I'm launching this Tuesday — would love your support 🚀

Hey [first name],

[1 personalized line — pick from the list below]

I've been quietly building TruNorth for the past year — a consumer
values shopping app that grades 11,000+ companies on what they
actually do, not what their marketing says.

Every grade traces back to a primary public record: FEC for political
donations, EPA for environmental violations, OSHA for worker safety,
SEC for executive pay. No vibes, no scraped opinions. Just receipts.

It launches on Product Hunt this Tuesday (June 23) at 2 AM CDT.

Two ways you can help me — pick whichever feels right:

1. Subscribe now so you get a launch-day push from PH:
   https://www.producthunt.com/products/trunorth?launch=trunorth

2. On Tuesday morning, if the product resonates with you, drop an
   upvote and maybe a comment. The first 4 hours on PH determine the
   whole day's rank — hour-1 votes count ~10x more than hour-12 votes.

If you want to poke around in TestFlight beforehand, just reply and
I'll send you an invite.

No pressure either way — just glad to have you in my corner.

Aron
Aron@trunorthapp.com
trunorthapp.com
```

### Personalization line ideas (line 1)

Match the right one to each recipient — this is the single highest-impact part of the email:

- **Family:** "Hope you and [partner/kid name] are doing well — quick favor coming."
- **Old colleague:** "I know it's been a minute — hope [project/company] is treating you well."
- **TestFlight tester:** "Thanks again for the [specific feedback they gave] — it shipped."
- **Mentor:** "Wanted you to be among the first people I told."
- **Friend who's launched something:** "You went through this with [their thing] — hoping you can return the favor."

**Don't skip this step.** Generic emails ("Hi friend!") convert ~3x worse than ones with a real personal line.
