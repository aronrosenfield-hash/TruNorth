### 8. Invite your connections to follow (5 min)

Page Admin → **Invite to follow** → select up to 100 connections per month.

LinkedIn replenishes credits monthly. Use them in waves:
- Week 1: closest 25 connections (family, close colleagues)
- Week 2: extended professional network (former coworkers)
- Week 3: industry contacts (product, design, conscious-consumer adjacent)
- Week 4: cold but relevant (people who post about ESG, ethical shopping)

---

### 9. Connect founder profile (2 min)

Edit your personal LinkedIn profile:
- **Current position**: Founder, TruNorth (link to the company page)
- **About** section: drop a 2-sentence mention of TruNorth
- **Featured** section: pin one TruNorth post

This creates a 2-way visibility loop — people on your personal feed see the company; people on the company page can find you as the founder.

---

### 10. Page-follower targets

Realistic milestones for an indie pre-launch:

| Milestone | Target |
|---|---|
| Day 1 (after upload) | 5-15 followers (you + immediate circle) |
| End of week 1 | 50 (after first invite wave) |
| End of week 4 (~launch) | 200-400 (cumulative invite waves + PH launch traffic) |
| End of Q3 2026 | 1,000+ (organic growth + post engagement) |

Don't chase follower count — chase **engagement rate**. A 50-follower page with 30% engagement beats a 5,000-follower page with 0.5%.

---

### 11. Ongoing cadence

- **1 post / week minimum** until launch
- **Daily during launch week (Jun 17-30)**
- **2-3 posts / week post-launch** for momentum
- Mix: 60% educational ("here's a primary source we use"), 30% product ("we shipped X"), 10% behind-the-scenes ("how I built this")
- Use Postiz (B-27, deferred) once you want one-click cross-posting to X + Threads + IG + Bluesky
