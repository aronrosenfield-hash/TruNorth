# Data-source discovery — net-new candidate sources (2026-06-28)

> Generated by a 19-agent deep-research sweep (16 sourcing lenses + inventory + synthesis + completeness critic). Inventory found **238** sources already in the pipeline; this sweep adds **113 distinct net-new** candidates (**66 confirmed commercial-license-OK**, 47 needing a license check). Paid-app rule: only ship sources with commercialUseOK=yes, or clear the license first (cf. the CC-BY-NC / Good Jobs First strips).

## Summary

From the 127-candidate sweep I deduplicated heavy repeats (World Bank debarred ×4, IDB ×3, GLEIF ×2, Open Ownership/BODS ×4, OpenCorporates ×2, FTC ×4, USDA Organic Integrity ×2, Transparentem ×2, TED ×2, LittleSis ×2, Open Supply Hub ×2) down to ~60 distinct sources and ranked them by leverage = impact × feasibility, with commercial-license-clean sources ranked above license-risky ones. The single highest-leverage layer is entity-resolution/ownership infrastructure (GLEIF CC0, UK Companies House PSC OGL, Open Ownership BODS CC0) — this is the spine that lets the existing 100+ sources attach to far more brands, especially non-US, which is TruNorth's biggest gap. The biggest clean non-US breadth wins are EU/UK/Canada government feeds with explicit open licenses: EU Safety Gate (RAPEX), EEA Industrial Emissions (E-PRTR), EU DSA Transparency DB, Health Canada Recalls, UK FSA Food Alerts, Canada Modern Slavery registry, EU TED, EUR-Lex/CELLAR, UK Find Case Law. The deepest clean new US signal categories are FTC enforcement (entire missing agency), FDA Warning Letters / inspection classifications / FAERS / import refusals (all openFDA CC0 or FDA public-domain), SEC AAERs, JPML mass-tort MDLs, CFPB enforcement actions, DOL OLMS persuader reports (unique union-busting signal), OSHA fatality abstracts, and the multilateral-development-bank debarment cluster (World Bank/IDB/AfDB/ADB — strong non-US anti-corruption). Critical license discipline: many high-quality NGO/watchdog datasets are CC-BY-NC, CC-BY-SA/ODbL ShareAlike, or unlicensed — the same trap that already forced the CC-BY-NC and Good Jobs First strips. UNEP Methane (BY-NC), GDPRhub (BY-NC), DuckDuckGo Tracker Radar (BY-NC-SA), Access to Nutrition (BY-NC-ND), ZSL SPOTT, Coller FAIRR, OCCRP Aleph, and Access to Medicine are NOT shippable in the paid app without a negotiated license. ShareAlike sources (Open Supply Hub, ICIJ Offshore Leaks, LittleSis, SourceWatch, Exodus, OpenCorporates free tier, Data Liberation APHIS parsed CSVs) are commercial-permitted-but-copyleft — usable only as derived binary facts re-sourced from primaries, never bulk-redistributed. For most watchdog compilations the safe pattern is: use as a LEAD, store only the non-copyrightable fact, and re-source from the primary public record (the GJF playbook). Recommended first integration is the WBA Data Explorer API (one wiring unlocks 6 CC-BY benchmarks covering ~2,000 global companies) plus GLEIF + Companies House PSC as the resolution backbone.

## Top priorities (integrate first)

1. GLEIF Global LEI Index (CC0) — entity-resolution + Level-2 parent graph; the spine that lets every other source attach to more brands, esp. non-US. Highest leverage, clean license.
2. WBA Data Explorer API (CC BY 4.0) — one wiring unlocks six benchmarks; the 2,000-company Social Benchmark is the widest clean global human-rights/social signal in the sweep.
3. EU Safety Gate / RAPEX (CC BY via data.europa.eu) — biggest single clean non-US product-safety breadth win; brand name is an explicit field.
4. EEA European Industrial Emissions Portal / E-PRTR (CC BY) — the EU analogue to EPA TRI/ECHO; thousands of non-US industrial parents become gradeable.
5. Health Canada Recalls + UK FSA Food Alerts API (both OGL, clean JSON, daily) — lowest-effort non-US safety/food feeds with well-structured brand fields.
6. FTC Cases & Proceedings + Refunds (US public domain) — an entire missing top-tier consumer-protection/privacy/advertising regulator, deep US signal.
7. openFDA cluster — Warning Letters + Inspection Classifications + FAERS + Import Refusals (CC0 / FDA public domain) — deep pre-recall pharma/food compliance + foreign-manufacturer breadth; pull from FDA.gov/openFDA, NOT the data.gov ODbL mirror.
8. UK Companies House PSC register + Open Ownership BODS (OGL / CC0) — beneficial-ownership backbone for UK + EU brands; pairs with GLEIF for the parent map.
9. World Bank + IDB MDB debarment cluster (WB public record / IDB CC BY) — strong non-US anti-corruption breadth; pull from the IGO primaries, never the OpenSanctions CC-BY-NC mirrors.
10. SEC AAERs + JPML MDLs + DOL OLMS Persuader Reports (all US public domain) — three deep, cheap, net-new US signals: accounting-fraud, mass-tort defendant status, and union-busting spend (a unique negative labor signal).

## Coverage gaps still thin

- NON-US ENFORCEMENT DEPTH still thin outside US/EU/UK/Canada/AU — Latin America, Africa, and Asia-Pacific company-specific conduct records are mostly reachable only via MDB debarment lists (WB/IDB/AfDB/ADB) and OCDS procurement; no broad LatAm/Africa/Asia consumer-conduct regulator feed is clean-licensed and ready.
- Beneficial-ownership / parent-resolution for PRIVATE non-US brands — GLEIF + UK PSC + Open Ownership cover UK/EU and LEI-holders well, but most private brands outside those registries still need OpenCorporates (paid, ShareAlike free tier) or registries not yet wired.
- EU/global PRIVACY-FINE signal is license-blocked — the richest sources (GDPRhub BY-NC, DuckDuckGo Tracker Radar BY-NC-SA) are NonCommercial; the clean path is scraping primary national DPA decision pages, which is unbuilt.
- WATER as a modality is essentially absent — only WRI Aqueduct (CC BY, but watershed-keyed, needs facility geocoding) was surfaced; no company-keyed water-conduct signal.
- FINANCED-HARM / banking-conduct depth is thin and mostly license-risky — BankTrack and ShareAction are unlicensed/uncertain; ECB/EDES cover EU prudential enforcement but the 'which companies a bank finances' signal lacks a clean source.
- DEFORESTATION / forest-risk commodity DEPTH is largely license-blocked — ZSL SPOTT (no, derivative-ban) and Trase (raw-data commercial ambiguous) are the best datasets but neither is clean for a paid app without an email/contract; only WBA Nature (CC BY) is clean.
- ANIMAL-WELFARE corporate-commitment breadth (ChickenWatch, EggTrack, Fur Free Retailer, FAIRR) is high-value but uniformly unlicensed/uncertain/no — coverage depends on re-sourcing each commitment fact from the company's own public statement (GJF pattern), which is labor-intensive.
- NUTRITION corporate-responsibility (ATNi) is CC-BY-NC-ND — no clean per-company nutrition-governance signal beyond product-level Open Food Facts already ingested.
- METHANE super-emitter attribution (UNEP MARS) is CC-BY-NC — no commercial-clean near-real-time methane-by-operator signal exists yet.
- STATE-LEVEL US enforcement is patchy and license-mixed — many of the richest state feeds (NY DFS, NY wage theft, WA AG via Socrata) lack explicit reuse grants; a systematic multi-state Socrata harvest is unbuilt.
- ISSUE-AD / dark-money spend (FCC OPIF, IRS 990 Schedule C) is clean-licensed but high-effort (OCR + free-text entity disambiguation + one-hop brand→trade-group mapping), so the indirect-influence modality remains under-exploited.
- IMMIGRATION-DETENTION & CARCERAL CONTRACTS — the list has USAspending generically but never treats ICE/DHS/BOP detention, GEO Group/CoreCivic, surveillance/e-carceration, and family-separation contracting as a distinct ethics modality. This is a top-of-mind values signal for many users and is fully covered by clean public records (USAspending award data + FPDS + DHS FOIA contract releases + ICE detention facility lists).
- WEAPONS / DEFENSE TIES beyond SIPRI's high-level arms-industry ranking — no nuclear-weapons-producer signal (Don't Bank on the Bomb / ICAN-PAX), no cluster-munition/landmine producer lists, no controversial-weapons screens, no DSCA Foreign Military Sales / DDTC export licensing, no UN Comtrade ML-category arms-trade attribution. 'Does this brand's parent make weapons?' is a core conscientious-objector filter that is essentially unserved.
- GAMBLING & PREDATORY FINANCE as a vice/harm category — no gambling-regulator enforcement (UK Gambling Commission public register is OGL-clean and rich; also state gaming commissions), no payday/title-lender or rent-to-own/BNPL predatory-lending enforcement beyond generic CFPB complaints, no debt-collector enforcement. Vice exposure (gambling, predatory credit) is a values dimension with zero dedicated source.
- MASS-LAYOFF / PLANT-CLOSURE conduct — WARN Act notices are public state records aggregating tens of thousands of companies and millions of affected workers, a direct labor-treatment signal entirely absent from the list (the labor stack is all wage/union/safety, never layoffs).
- LATIN AMERICA / AFRICA / ASIA GOVERNMENT ENFORCEMENT DEPTH — the research team self-identified this gap but then only reached for MDB debarment + OCDS. There ARE clean, company-keyed national regulator feeds: Brazil IBAMA environmental fines/embargoes (open data), Chile SNIFA (full public environmental-sanction system), Mexico PROFEPA, South Africa Competition Commission/Tribunal, India NGT / SEBI / NCLT, Indonesia/Malaysia environmental ministries. These move non-US brands across the gradeable threshold.
- COMPANY-KEYED CLIMATE/CARBON COMPLIANCE outside the US/EU-PRTR — the EU ETS Union Registry / EUTL gives per-installation VERIFIED emissions tied to named account-holder companies (15,000+ stationary installations) with a 3-year delay; it's distinct from EEA E-PRTR and from voluntary CDP/SBTi, and is EEA-licensed for commercial reuse. A mandatory-disclosure carbon-conduct signal for thousands of EU operators is missing.
- CORPORATE LEAK / WHISTLEBLOWER ARCHIVES beyond ICIJ Offshore Leaks — Distributed Denial of Secrets (DDoSecrets) curates 100M+ files of corporate leaks (data breaches, internal docs, lobbying); usable only as investigative LEADS re-sourced to primary public records (the GJF playbook), but completely untapped as a discovery layer.
- AGGRESSIVE TAX-AVOIDANCE / PROFIT-SHIFTING signal — the list has SEC effective tax rate + ITEP (US) only. Public Country-by-Country Reporting (EU public CbCR live from 2025, plus Australia's public CbCR) and the OECD aggregated CbCR statistics expose multinational profit-shifting at the entity level; Tax Justice Network's Corporate Tax Haven Index frames jurisdiction risk. A tax-fairness modality with global reach is under-built.
- SATELLITE / NEAR-REAL-TIME DEFORESTATION attributable to companies — the team flagged deforestation depth as license-blocked (SPOTT, Trase), but Global Forest Watch's Data API (GLAD/RADD alerts + concession/mill ownership layers) is explicitly CC BY 4.0, commercial-clean, and can be facility/concession-geocoded to operators. A clean, dynamic deforestation-by-company source was overlooked.
- EMERGING EU MANDATORY-DISCLOSURE FIREHOSE — the European Single Access Point (ESAP) will centralize CSRD/ESRS sustainability statements, CSDDD due-diligence reports and ESEF financials in machine-readable XBRL under EU public-domain reuse rules (phased 2026-2028). Worth pre-wiring as the single biggest future breadth+depth unlock for EU brands; the list never mentions ESAP.
